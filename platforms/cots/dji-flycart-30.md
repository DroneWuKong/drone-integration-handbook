# DJI FlyCart 30

> **Category:** Commercial / Industrial — Heavy-Lift Delivery
> **NDAA Status:** NOT compliant
> **Manufacturer:** DJI (Shenzhen, China)

---

## Overview

DJI's heavy-lift delivery drone. 4-axis, 8-propeller configuration. 30 kg payload (dual battery) / 40 kg (single battery). Cargo mode and winch mode (30 m retractable cable). FAA-approved with Remote ID in the US. Built for logistics, agriculture supply, emergency response, and industrial material transport.

## Gotchas

1. **NOT NDAA compliant.**
2. **Winch kit sold separately** — base price does not include it.
3. **Battery and charger sold separately.**
4. **Requires careful weight/CG management** — drone auto-detects payload weight and center of gravity.
5. **FlyCart 100 (80 kg payload) exists but is China-only** as of 2026.
