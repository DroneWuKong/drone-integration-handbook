# DJI Agras T50

> **Category:** Commercial / Industrial — Agriculture
> **NDAA Status:** NOT compliant
> **Manufacturer:** DJI (Shenzhen, China)

---

## Overview

DJI's flagship agricultural spraying/spreading drone. Coaxial twin-rotor design carrying 40 kg spraying or 50 kg spreading payloads. Dual atomizing spraying system, front/rear phased array radars, binocular vision. The dominant platform in commercial ag spraying worldwide. Covers ~52 acres/hour at 2 GPA.

## Gotchas

1. **NOT NDAA compliant.** Federal ag funding that requires compliant equipment cannot be used for this platform.
2. **Requires Part 137 exemption** for commercial agricultural application in the US.
3. **88 lb empty weight** — not remotely portable. Requires truck/trailer setup.
4. **DJI FlyCart 100 and T60 exist in China but are NOT FAA approved** in the US. The T50 is currently the largest DJI ag drone available in the US market.
5. **Battery logistics are critical.** 9-min fast charge with DJI C10000 charger + generator is the only way to maintain production rates.
6. **Nozzle clogging with powder-base products** was an issue on earlier models — dual atomizing centrifugal nozzles on T50 address this.
