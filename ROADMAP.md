# Roadmap

Future chapters, sections, and improvements under consideration.
Open an issue to suggest additions or volunteer to write one.

## Planned Chapters

- [ ] **Chapter 16: Electronic Warfare Awareness** — What jamming and spoofing look like from the operator's perspective. Detection, mitigation, operational planning in contested spectrum.
- [ ] **Chapter 17: Payload Integration Patterns** — Mapping cameras, multispectral sensors, LiDAR, delivery mechanisms. Wiring, power, data flow, and the companion computer as integration hub.
- [ ] **Chapter 18: Cellular / LTE for BVLOS** — When and how to use cellular modems for beyond-visual-line-of-sight. Hardware, SIM management, latency reality, regulatory considerations.
- [ ] **Chapter 19: Fixed-Wing Specific** — Airspeed sensors, VTOL transitions, iNav vs ArduPilot for wings, long-range planning.
- [ ] **Chapter 20: Fleet Management** — Managing 5-50 platforms. Configuration management, firmware versioning, battery tracking, maintenance schedules.
- [ ] **Chapter 21: Power Systems Deep Dive** — Battery chemistry, BEC selection, power distribution boards, voltage sag under load, field charging.
- [ ] **Chapter 22: Ground Control Stations** — Mission Planner, QGroundControl, ATAK plugins, custom GCS options. What works, what doesn't, and the tradeoffs.
